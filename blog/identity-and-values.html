Identity and Values

Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make.
Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made and the consequences of
the action you took.

I use to work in a wholesale supermarket and one day i discovered that an entire pallet of chicken breast
had expired on the weekend, while the weekend staff should have caught onto this, it was my mistake for not
checking the short date on the friday.

I had the choice to either keep quiet, freeze the chicken and sell it later or
to discard it and cost the company. I chose to discard the chicken as it was a health and safety risk and
did not want to comprismise my integrity.

The consequences of this decision was a mild telling off, and while it cost the company a few grand of lost stock it
it eventually led to a consistent date checking process as well as an extra staff member working the same department

Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others,
but there was resistance or tension. Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your
reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why.

disagreements about the arrangements there was a ridiculous amount of "noodling". This eventually led to tensions
